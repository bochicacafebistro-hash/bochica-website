      About the Aguardiente Antioqueno Sin Azucar
      Aguardiente antionqueño is an alcoholic beverage from Colombia. The word Aguardiente (Firewater) is a compound word combining the Latin words for Water and Fiery and the word Antioqueño is derived from its region Antioquia. Its produced with the extra pure natural essence of Anise and Sugar cane with an alcohol content of 29% with the very first scent through to the crisp cessation, the tasty anise is premium, and taken usually as a shot but also to make creating delicious cocktails.
